VisitorFieldValidator允许将校验转移给活动的属性并使用它自己的校验文件. This allows you to use the ModelDriven development pattern and manage your validations for your models in one place, where they belong, next to your model classes. The VisitorFieldValidator can handle either simple Object properties, Collections of Objects, or Arrays. See XW:Standard Validators#VisitorFieldValidator
